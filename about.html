About


I have a college degree in electronics and a diverse professional background spanning I.T., video production, and now the audiovisual industry, where I work as a Technical Director. As a hobbyist, I'm passionate about digital electronics, retro computing, and custom hardware projects. I love sharing my work through YouTube videos, blog posts, and open-source contributions.

My past projects range from microcontroller-driven devices to retro homebrew computers based on the 65C02, Z80, and 6809/6309. I love programming these classic CPUs in assembly and am excited to customize my next project to work with NitrOS-9 OEU edition—my most challenging build yet.

Follow me on YouTube and Blogger to join the adventure!
