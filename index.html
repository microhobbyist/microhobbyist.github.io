About Me

I have a college degree in electronics and a diverse professional background spanning I.T., video production, and now the audiovisual industry, where I work as a Technical Director. As an electronics hobbyist, I'm passionate about digital electronics, retro computing, and custom hardware projects. I love sharing my work through YouTube videos, blog posts, and open-source contributions.

Projects

From breadboard prototyping to designing custom PCBs and programming PLDs, I explore a wide range of digital electronics topics. My current project, the 6309-Unleashed computer build, features a custom keyboard, an MMU with 2MB RAM, a V9958 graphics card, multichannel sound, and possibly a floppy drive and IDE hard drive. Oh, and let's not forget a network adapter.
